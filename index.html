About Incubill

Incubill is a PDF estimate generator built for tradespeople — plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, landscapers, handymen, cleaners, and general contractors — who need to send a client a professional, itemized quote in minutes instead of an hour.

Subscriptions are billed and processed by Dodo Payments, acting as Merchant of Record, with a 7-day free trial on every plan. Your payment details are handled entirely by Dodo's checkout — this page never sees or stores card information directly.

The tool runs entirely in your browser: your client's name, address, and job details are never sent to or stored on an external server, aside from the one-time location lookup used to guess a starting currency (which you can always override).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Incubill free to use?

Yes, for the first 7 days. Every subscription includes a free trial with full access, billed afterward through Dodo Payments unless you cancel first.

Which trades does Incubill support?

Plumbing, electrical, HVAC, landscaping, handyman work, cleaning services, and general contracting are built in, each with its own sample line items. The estimate math and PDF layout work identically for any trade.

Does Incubill work in currencies other than US dollars?

Yes. Incubill detects the visitor's local currency automatically and lets anyone switch to a different one manually, including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, AUD, CAD, JPY, AED, ZAR, and SGD.

Can I send the estimate directly to a client?

Yes. After generating the PDF, you can share it straight to WhatsApp, email, or your device's native share sheet, in addition to downloading it.

Do I need to install anything to use Incubill?

No. Incubill runs entirely in your web browser — there's nothing to download or install to create and send an estimate.
